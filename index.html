DREAMSCAPE MEDIA GROUP are a Artistic collective comprised of MC's, Producers / Beatmakers,Visual Artists, Videographers and Photographers.

We are Dedicated to upholding Artistic merit and Integrity in all forms and hold Substance and Depth at their highest values. DREAMSCAPE is a coalition of outcasts,radical thinkers, free minds and visionaries.

We are inclusive and against all forms of oppression including racism (systemically and otherwise) ,sexism, homophobia ,transphobia, rape culture and mental illness discrimination. We are made up of members of your community and come from all walks of Life. DREAMSCAPE's Mission is to bring Creativity to the forefront in a space where shock value reigns over originality. We are Thinkers. We are Doers. We are Makers.
